ABOUT ME

Transforming ideas into digital experience

Hello, I'm Bidhata. I’m currently learning front-end web development using HTML, CSS, and Tailwind CSS, and I’m loving the process of turning ideas into real websites.

My goal is to become a confident developer who can build beautiful, responsive sites from scratch. I may be just starting out, but I’m constantly learning and experimenting with new ideas. Right now, I’m focused on getting comfortable with layout, design, and responsive web pages. In the future, I plan to expand my skills by learning JavaScript and diving into UI/UX design.
